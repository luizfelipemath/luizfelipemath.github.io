About me

My name is Luiz. I'm a PhD student in Mathematics at the Department of Mathematics of the Federal University of Paraíba (DM-UFPB), in Brazil, under the supervision of Prof. João Marcos do Ó. I am currently supported by a CAPES scholarship. My main research interests lie in:

  • Partial Differential Equations
  • Geometric Analysis

Previously, I earned a bachelor's degree at IME-USP under the supervision of Prof. Paulo D. Cordaro with the support of a FAPESP grant. During this period, I also spent one semester at the Technical University of Munich, in Germany.
